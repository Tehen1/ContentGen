About FixieNFTRun

FixieNFTRun is revolutionizing the cycling experience by combining blockchain technology, NFTs, and fitness. Our platform allows cyclists to earn rewards, collect unique NFT bikes, and be part of a thriving community.

  • Earn $FIXIE tokens while cycling
  • Collect and trade unique NFT bikes
  • Participate in challenges and competitions
  • Connect with a global community of cyclists

Join the cycling revolution!

How It Works

1. Ride Your Bike

Use the FixieNFTRun app to track your cycling activities and earn $FIXIE tokens based on your performance.

2. Earn Rewards

Accumulate $FIXIE tokens for every kilometer you ride. The more you cycle, the more you earn!

3. Collect NFTs

Use your earned tokens to mint unique NFT bikes, each with its own attributes and rarity levels.
