What is FixieNFTRun?

FixieNFTRun is a revolutionary Web3 application that combines the thrill of fixed-gear cycling with blockchain technology and NFTs. Our platform allows riders to earn rewards for their cycling activities, turning their passion into profit.

Key features of FixieNFTRun include:

  • NFT Fixie Bikes: Unique, digital representations of fixed-gear bicycles
  • Move-to-Earn Mechanics: Earn cryptocurrency by cycling in the real world
  • Urban Exploration: Discover new routes and earn bonuses for exploring your city
  • Community Challenges: Compete with other riders in various cycling events
  • Upgradeable NFTs: Improve your digital bike's performance over time

Join us in revolutionizing urban transportation and promoting a healthier, more sustainable lifestyle!

How It Works

1. Mint Your NFT Fixie

Create your unique digital fixed-gear bike. Each NFT Fixie has its own attributes and performance characteristics.

2. Ride & Earn

Connect your smartphone, start cycling, and earn cryptocurrency rewards based on your distance, speed, and routes.

3. Upgrade & Compete

Use your earnings to upgrade your NFT Fixie, participate in challenges, and climb the leaderboards.
