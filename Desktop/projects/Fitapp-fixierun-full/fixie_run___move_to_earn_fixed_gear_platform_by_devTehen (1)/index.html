Everything you need to know about Fixie.Run

Fixie.Run is specifically designed for fixed gear cycling culture. We understand track stands, skids, messenger runs, and the unique aspects of riding without freewheels. Our rewards system recognizes authentic fixed gear techniques.

Using advanced motion sensors and machine learning, we detect when you're performing a track stand by analyzing balance patterns, micro-movements, and duration. Perfect stands earn bonus rewards!

Fixie.Run is optimized for fixed gear bikes. Our algorithms detect fixed gear riding patterns through pedal cadence, braking patterns, and movement signatures that are unique to fixies.

As you accumulate miles, your NFT bike gains new components, visual upgrades, and performance boosts. Each evolution reflects real fixed gear culture - from basic track builds to premium pursuit setups.
